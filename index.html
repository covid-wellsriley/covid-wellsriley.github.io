Disclaimer

Wells-Riley is a mathematical model which factors breathing rates, quanta production, ventilation, and room size into estimating infection risk. The statistical model based on a Poisson distribution can only estimate infection risk, R, which cannot be guaranteed in real life applications. The major limitation of Wells-Riley is the assumption of perfect mixing which is not a reasonable assumption for real life infection transmission. This model is meant to be used for estimation purposes and not definitive calculations.

About

This app uses the Wells-Riley model to simulate the infection risk of various airborne pathogens considering ventilation. This calculator determines the number of Philips air disinfection devices required to achieve an infection list of less than 1 and 0.25 persons in addition to a 99.99% pathogen removal efficiency. Please note that this model assumes perfect mixing.
